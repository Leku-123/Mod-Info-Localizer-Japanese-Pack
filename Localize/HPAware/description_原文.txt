This is a simple client-side mod that contains:

- Hurt and low HP/Health overlays
- Player HP bar
- Audio and visual indicator above you whenever potion sickness wears off
- Debuff indicators above you whenever you obtain a debuff
- Extra, optional features such as audio indicators, gray vision, and more

Each feature is highly customizable & can be toggled on/off in the config.

Suggest features, report bugs, or see preview images on the Steam page.
